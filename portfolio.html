Residential Tree Planting Program Dashboard

Created a dashboard for the City of College Park, to help with the efforts to disseminate information about their Residential Tree Planting Program. Cleaned, and prepped the data with Python, and also added a carbon captured metric.

Interactive map for the City of College Park. The tool was built with ArcGIS Experience Builder and aggregates data from county and city level.

Custom script tool developed with ArcPy to analyze crash data and aggregate incidents by road. The tool outputs road segments classified by crash or fatalities hotspots.
